### 您主动提供的数据 / Data You Provide

为提供薪资计算功能，您可以在应用内配置以下信息：

- 月薪金额
- 每月工作天数
- 每日工作时间（上班/下班时间）
- 休息时间段
- 发薪日
- 税率

**以上所有信息完全由您自主选择是否填写，仅存储在您的设备本地。**

To provide the salary calculation functionality, You may configure the following information within the app:

- Monthly pay amount
- Working days per month
- Daily work schedule (start/end times)
- Break segments
- Pay day
- Tax rate

**All of the above information is entirely voluntary, and is stored solely on Your device locally.**

### 使用数据 / Usage Data

本应用**不收集任何使用数据**。我们没有集成任何分析框架、崩溃报告工具或使用统计服务。应用不会向外部服务器发送任何网络请求。

Sakipay **does not collect any Usage Data**. We do not integrate any analytics frameworks, crash reporting tools, or usage statistics services. The app makes no network requests to any external servers.

## 信息的使用 / Use of Your Information

我们仅将您提供的信息用于以下目的：

- **提供核心功能**：根据您配置的薪资参数，实时计算并展示您的今日收入、月收入摘要和工作进度。
- **数据持久化**：将您的配置保存在设备本地，以便您下次打开应用时无需重新设置。

We use the information You provide solely for the following purposes:

- **To provide core functionality**: To calculate and display Your real-time earnings, monthly summary, and work progress based on the salary parameters You configure.
- **Data persistence**: To save Your configuration locally on Your device so You do not need to re-enter it each time You open the app.

## 信息的存储与安全 / Data Storage & Security

**所有数据仅存储在您的设备本地。** 我们不为本应用运营任何服务器，不拥有任何后台基础设施。您的薪资配置数据保存在您设备的本地存储中（iOS 使用 SwiftData 和 UserDefaults，HarmonyOS 使用 preferences）。

我们不收集、不接收、不处理任何形式的您的个人信息。数据完全由您掌控。

**All data is stored locally on Your device only.** We do not operate any servers or backend infrastructure for this app. Your salary configuration data is stored in Your device's local storage (SwiftData and UserDefaults on iOS, preferences on HarmonyOS).

We do not collect, receive, or process any of Your personal information in any form. You have full control over Your data.

## 信息的共享与披露 / Sharing & Disclosure of Your Information

**我们不会与任何第三方共享、出售、转让或披露您的个人信息。**

由于所有数据仅存储在您的设备本地而我们不拥有任何服务器，因此不存在数据被上传、传输或共享给他人的技术可能性。

我们可能在以下情况下披露信息：

- **法律要求**：如果法律要求或应公共当局的有效请求（例如法院或政府机构），我们可能需要披露信息。但鉴于我们实际上不持有您的任何数据，此类披露在技术上不可行。

**We do not share, sell, transfer, or disclose Your personal information to any third party.**

Since all data is stored locally on Your device and We do not operate any servers, there is no technical possibility for data to be uploaded, transferred, or shared with others.

We may disclose information in the following situation:

- **Legal requirements**: We may be required to disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency). However, since We do not actually hold any of Your data, such disclosure is technically not feasible.

## 信息的保留与删除 / Data Retention & Deletion

### 保留 / Retention

您的信息仅在您使用本应用期间保留在您的设备上。我们不在任何服务器上保留您的任何个人信息。

Your information is retained on Your device only while You use the app. We do not retain any of Your personal information on any server.

### 删除 / Deletion

您可以通过以下方式删除您的数据：

- **应用内删除**：在应用设置中清除或修改您已配置的信息。
- **卸载应用**：卸载应用将彻底删除您设备上所有由本应用存储的数据。本应用不使用 iCloud 同步或任何其他云存储方案，因此卸载即意味着所有数据的完全清除。

You may delete Your data in the following ways:

- **In-app deletion**: Clear or modify Your configured information within the app settings.
- **App uninstallation**: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all data stored by this app on Your device. The app does not use iCloud sync or any other cloud storage, so uninstallation means complete removal of all data.
